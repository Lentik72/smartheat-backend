Supplier FAQ

Is there really no fee?

Correct. Listing is free. We believe comprehensive supplier coverage makes the platform more useful for homeowners, which is our priority.

How do you make money?

HomeHeat is currently funded by the founder. Future monetization may include optional promotional tools (clearly disclosed to homeowners), but basic listing will remain free.

Can I update my price daily?

Yes. Once your listing is verified, text your price to (845) 335-8855 or use your secure link. First-time texts require a one-time YES confirmation. After that, updates go live within minutes.

What if I don't see my business listed?

Email us at support@gethomeheat.com and we'll add you to the directory.

Do I need to respond to leads quickly?

No. There are no response time requirements or penalties. Run your business your way.

Can I list on HomeHeat and other sites?

Absolutely. We don't require exclusivity. Use whatever marketing channels work for your business.
