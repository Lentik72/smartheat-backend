Frequently Asked Questions

How does HomeHeat estimate when I'll run out?

HomeHeat uses your logged tank readings, recent usage patterns, and local weather data to estimate how long your fuel supply will last.

Do I need a smart tank sensor?

No. HomeHeat works with manual gauge readings and does not require any hardware or sensors.

Does HomeHeat work with propane?

Yes! HomeHeat supports both heating oil and propane tanks. Just select your fuel type in settings.

Is HomeHeat free?

Yes. HomeHeat is completely free to download and use on iOS. No subscriptions, no ads, no hidden costs.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data stays on your device. HomeHeat does not require an account and does not sell or share your information.
