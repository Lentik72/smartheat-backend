Program Name

HomeHeat Supplier Price Updates

Company

HomeHeat is operated by Tsoir Advisors LLC.

What This Service Does

HomeHeat allows heating oil suppliers to update their listed price by sending a text message to (845) 335-8855. When you text your price, it is published on your supplier listing at gethomeheat.com. This service is free for suppliers.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You will receive a reply each time you text us a price update. We may occasionally send a reminder if your listed price has not been updated in over 7 days. You can expect no more than a few messages per week.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-In

By texting your price to (845) 335-8855, you consent to receive SMS replies from HomeHeat related to your supplier listing. Your first price update requires a one-time confirmation (reply YES) before publishing.

Opt-Out

Text STOP to (845) 335-8855 at any time to stop receiving messages. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed. After opting out, your existing listed price will remain until it expires.

Help

Text HELP to (845) 335-8855 for assistance, or email support@gethomeheat.com.

What Data We Collect

When you text us, we store your phone number and message content to match you to your supplier listing and process your price update. We do not share your phone number with third parties or use it for marketing purposes.

Supported Carriers

Compatible with all major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and others. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
