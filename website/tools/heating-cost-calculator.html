Data sources & methodology
  • Oil prices: Local median from HomeHeat's daily supplier scrapes
  • Electricity & gas rates: EIA residential averages by state (updated monthly)
  • Heating degree days: NOAA 30-year Climate Normals by county
  • Home size: Assumes 2,000 sq ft with typical insulation (7 BTU/sq-ft/HDD)
  • Heat pump COP: 3.0 (cold-climate mini-split average)
  • Heating season: 6 months (October–March)

Actual costs depend on home insulation, equipment age, thermostat settings, and weather. Use these estimates as a starting point for comparing fuel options.
