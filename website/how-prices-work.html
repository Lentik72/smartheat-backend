Important: Prices Are Estimates

Published prices are starting points. Final price may vary based on delivery quantity, payment method, location, and current market conditions. Always confirm pricing when you call.

How Listings Are Ordered

To help you make an informed choice, we prioritize suppliers who provide transparent, recently updated pricing:

  • 1. Verified Pricing First. Suppliers with prices updated within the last 24-48 hours appear first, sorted from lowest to highest price.
  • 2. Location & Coverage. We then list suppliers who serve your specific ZIP code but may require a phone call for a current quote.
  • 3. Future Signals. As we grow, factors like availability and response history will also influence ordering.

Why Price Transparency Matters

Suppliers who publish current prices are easier to compare. Those without prices aren't necessarily worse‚Äîthey may prefer to quote based on your specific needs. We show both so you can choose.

What We Don't Do

  • No paid placement. Suppliers can't pay to appear higher in search results.
  • No hidden algorithms. We don't secretly demote suppliers based on proprietary scores.
  • No exclusivity requirements. Suppliers can list on HomeHeat alongside any other platform.
  • No response time penalties. We don't punish suppliers for being busy or slow to respond.
  • No lead interception. When you call a supplier, you're calling them directly. We don't record calls or insert ourselves into the relationship.

Price Accuracy & Freshness

We indicate when prices were last updated so you can judge their reliability:

  • Today/Yesterday: Very fresh. Likely accurate.
  • This week: Recent. Should be close.
  • Older: May have changed. Confirm when calling.

Prices in the heating oil market can change daily based on wholesale costs, so we encourage you to verify the current price when you contact a supplier.
